Hi, I'm
Kasper.

I'm a motion designer with a deep interest in 3D and pushing software to its limits — especially Blender and its geometry nodes, which play a central role in both my personal and client work.

A lot of my spare time goes into exploring and developing my skills, which traces back to my background in music. The two worlds have merged in my work creating visuals for live performances, where what happens on stage and what happens on screen feed into each other and become something bigger.

Category — 01

Music visuals

Working alongside musicians to build visual worlds for their sound is one of the most expressive and experimental spaces I get to inhabit. Every project is its own universe.

01

kingtommi — Merlins

This project needed animated sequences for the intro where an old book tells the origin story of the song's protagonist and introduces the villain of the music video. When the characters travel, we made Lord of the Rings-esque map sequences to better merge different locations.

The shots were created in Blender, incorporating grease pencil for the hand-drawn illustration sequences shown below.

Blender Eevee Grease Pencil

Animated sequences

02

Neon Priest — Forever

The band Neon Priest wanted to create a promo video for social media for their new song "Forever".

The shots were created using Blender and was a great exercise in making controlled chaos with different simulations and dynamic camera angles. Below you can see the visuals displayed at Roskilde Festival during their performance on the EOS stage.

Blender After Effects Simulation

Photographs from Roskilde Festival

03

Ungklang — Roskilde Festival

The choir group Ungklang wanted an EP cover for their new release called "Lys". Furthermore they wanted concert visuals for their whole performance at Roskilde Festival 2025 and it was a blast!

The shots were created using Blender and TouchDesigner — a great exercise in making controlled chaos with different simulations that respond to input from a MIDI-controller and sound from the stage.

Blender TouchDesigner Resolume Arena MIDI

Recordings from Roskilde Festival

Category — 02

Motion Design

Motion design lives at the intersection of graphic design, animation, and narrative. These are projects where the movement itself carries the message — from brand identity to digital products.

01

Den Fantastiske Bus

Conceptual and R&D development for Skjaldborn, referencing Den Fantastiske Bus (Gyldendal) by Jakob Martin Strid.

This project was an exploration of how the beloved children's book could be animated with a mix of 2D and 3D.

Blender R&D

02

Papirprinsessen

My first exam at the Danish School of Media and Journalism was a digital children's book about the human body. Our solution was to follow the journey of "Papirprinsessen" exploring a world made up of cardboard, tape and colored markers.

From just 3 weeks of work I am very proud of how far we got with this project — I got to focus on creating the universe in 3D along with some cute animations.

Blender After Effects
Read more on Behance

Animated sequences

03

While In Battle I Am Free

Promotion video for a dance performance by the same name. Animated sequences introduce the protagonist and build the visual world of the piece.

Created in Blender using the Eevee render engine, with grease pencil elements woven throughout.

Blender Eevee Grease Pencil

04

Motion Design with Sonic College

This was a school project at DMJX where we collaborated with students from Sonic College to create a motion design piece.

It was a really fun and rewarding experience working with people that are so deep into sound and being more aware of making design choices that had sound in mind.

Blender

05

DoorDing

This project was made for DoorDing to illustrate their concept of making digital doorbells that let you control them from your phone.

It was my first attempt at properly making characters in After Effects and animating them with rigs.

After Effects Illustrator Character Rigging

06

Auwau — Backup-as-a-service Explainer

The biggest challenge with this project was explaining digital infrastructure in a simple and understandable way. This is where I really started playing with 3D in After Effects and to this day I am still pretty satisfied with how some of the sequences turned out.

After Effects Illustrator

07

Iværksætterpuljen

This project was made for Erhvervshus Sjælland to showcase how small businesses can apply for funding with a step-by-step guide.

A further exercise in character animation and rigging in After Effects, pushing the complexity of the rig further than the DoorDing project.

After Effects Illustrator Character Rigging
