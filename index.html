Category — 01

Music visuals

Working alongside musicians to build visual worlds for their sound is one of the most expressive and experimental spaces I get to inhabit. Every project is its own universe.

Project image

01

kingtommi — Merlins

This project called for animated sequences steeped in high fantasy — drawing deep from the visual language of Lord of the Rings and Game of Thrones to build a world that felt ancient and mythic.

All shots were created in Blender using the Eevee render engine, allowing for real-time shading and dramatic atmospheric lighting throughout.

Blender Eevee 3D Animation
Project image

02

Neon Priest — Forever

A project built around contrast and neon-drenched atmosphere. The brief was to create visuals that sat between industrial darkness and vivid, almost euphoric color — a tension that lives in the music itself.

Motion design and compositing were handled in After Effects, with 3D elements rendered in Cinema 4D.

After Effects Cinema 4D Compositing
